World of Happiness

This visualization will give viewers an overview of the interesting, livable and happiness of all countries around the world. The datasets and ranking we have is between the period 2015 - 2019. The score of happiness in each country will be calculated by combine the score of 6 different aspects : GDP score, Social support, Healthy life expectency, Freedom to make life choices, Perceptions of corruption and Generosity of people. Hope that my visualization will help viewers answer questions they expect, like 'How is the country he is living in on the ranking of the most livable countries in the world?'
